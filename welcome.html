Tips for best results

Lighting
Natural, even lighting works best—avoid harsh shadows.
Focus
Make sure the leaf is sharp and centered in the frame.
Background
Use a plain background if possible to reduce distractions.
Angles
Capture the diseased area clearly; multiple angles help when uncertain.
